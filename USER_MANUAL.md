## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Do I need to login to browse the directory?**
A: No! The directory is public and anyone can browse without logging in.

**Q: Can I update my own profile?**
A: Contact an administrator with your updated information.

**Q: How do I find someone specific?**
A: Use the search bar on the Directory page or ask the AI chat assistant.

**Q: Are photos downloadable?**
A: Some galleries may allow downloads. Check with administrators.

---

### AI Chat Questions

**Q: How does the AI know about alumni?**
A: Administrators upload alumni profile data to the AI system. It reads this information and answers your questions.

**Q: Can I chat about anything?**
A: The AI is trained to answer questions about Kenavo alumni. It works best for queries about classmates, their jobs, locations, etc.

**Q: Is my conversation private?**
A: Conversations are stored temporarily during your session. They're not linked to your personal account if you're not logged in.

**Q: Why is the AI slow sometimes?**
A: The AI needs to search through documents and generate responses, which takes 2-5 seconds. If it's very slow, there might be high traffic.

**Q: Can I see who else is chatting with AI?**
A: No, each chat session is private.

---

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browsers work best?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge all work great. Mobile browsers also work well.

**Q: Why can't I see some images?**
A: Check your internet connection. If problems persist, contact administrators.

**Q: The chat widget disappeared. How do I get it back?**
A: Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R). The purple button should reappear at the bottom-right.

**Q: I'm getting an error message. What should I do?**
A: Try refreshing the page first. If the error persists, contact administrators through the contact form.
